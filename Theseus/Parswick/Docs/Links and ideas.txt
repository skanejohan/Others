You are Fiona McDougal, 45 yrs old with a 5 year old son at home. You want him to take over the family business if it manages to survive. Your major goal is to avoid bancruptcy. This will be indicated when talking to your uncle Ailbeart.

IDEAS
- Need money to keep the old bokstore running. Will find a secret passageway from the office down to underground stuff.
- As you go along, you will need information from different books of different sections. 
- Ghost of some early decesed aunt from the 19th century (or early 20th). She will help translate / interpret some information.
- Goal is to find the family treasure.
- idag - ska hänga porträtt av tidigare ägare som man sedan kan möta tidigare.

- 1977 - (nu är kombinationen födelseår 1973), måste få ägaren att göra något som förändrar vad som finns i källaren idag.
